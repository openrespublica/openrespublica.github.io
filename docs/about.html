Initiative Profile

[cite_start]OpenResPublica is a national-ready digital governance documentation suite designed for Barangays, Municipalities, Cities, and Provincial Governments[cite: 39]. Unlike traditional tech providers that prioritize proprietary software, OpenResPublica prioritizes legal sovereignty.

[cite_start]We provide a unified, legally validated, and operationally practical model that any LGU can adopt regardless of size or resources[cite: 148]. [cite_start]Our framework aligns strictly with existing laws including the 1987 Constitution, the Local Government Code of 1991, and COA Circulars on digital records[cite: 41].
